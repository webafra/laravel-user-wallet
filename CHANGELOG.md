## v2.0.0 - 2026-03-11

### ✨ Improvements

This release introduces major improvements to the wallet payment system with a focus on better architecture, performance, and flexibility.

#### Key Changes

* **Refactored `pay` method** to improve object-oriented design and maintainability.
* **Performance optimizations** in the payment workflow.
* Added the ability to **select a specific wallet** to deduct the payment from.
* Implemented a **balance validation step** before attempting to deduct from multiple wallets.
* Improved the logic for **handling payments across multiple wallets**.
* Cleaner and more extensible internal structure for future features.

These changes make the payment process more reliable, efficient, and easier to extend.
